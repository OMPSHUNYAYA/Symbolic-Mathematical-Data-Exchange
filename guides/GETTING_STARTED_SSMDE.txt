GETTING STARTED — Shunyaya Symbolic Mathematical Data Exchange (SSMDE)

Observation-only. Values unchanged. Accountability added.

**What is SSMDE (in one line)**
Carry five inseparable parts with every signal: **value**, **align**, **band**, **manifest_id**, **stamp**.

**Why it matters**
- **Operators protected.** Every declaration says how sure it is and which rulebook defined that judgment.
- **Policy becomes provable.** Decisions are tied to manifest versions you can audit.
- **Trust travels.** Any system can verify the same five parts without custom glue.

----------------------------------------
DAY 1 — Minimal, no-dashboards-broken rollout
----------------------------------------
**Objective:** Emit SSMDE alongside your existing payloads (no removals). Keep dashboards running.

**Minimal record shape** (five canonical fields; your own schema around it is OK):
`{
  "value": { ...original measurements/signals... },
  "align": <float in (-1,+1)>,
  "band": "<human label>",
  "manifest_id": "<policy identifier>",
  "stamp": "SSMCLOCK1|<utc_iso>|theta=<deg>|sha256=<hex>|prev=<hex-or-NONE>"
}`

**How to compute `align`** (canonical pipeline; keep as-is):
`a_c := clamp(a_raw, -1+eps_a, +1-eps_a)`
`u := atanh(a_c)`
`U += w*u ; W += w`
`align := tanh( U / max(W, eps_w) )`
- **a_raw** = your domain’s raw stability/uncertainty/stress/confidence samples (windowed or point list).
- **eps_a** ~ `1e-6` (bound |a| < 1). **eps_w** ~ `1e-12` (avoid divide-by-zero).
- **w** default = 1 per sample (or pass weights).

**Band mapping**
- A **manifest** defines cutpoints and action windows, e.g. CRITICAL < AMBER < A0 < A++.
- Your `align` falls into exactly one band. Keep labels simple and human.

**Stamp**
- Build over **(value, align, band, manifest_id)** only.
- Format: `SSMCLOCK1|<utc_iso>|theta=<0..360>|sha256=<canonical_json_hex>|prev=<hash(prev_stamp)|NONE>`
- `prev` chains your declarations (timeline visible by design).

**Try it now (local)**
1) `python ssmde_align_core.py --demo --pretty`
2) `python ssmde_align_core.py --emit-manifest "MANIFEST_SAMPLE.json"`
3) `python ssmde_align_core.py --emit-examples "SSMDE_RECORD_EXAMPLES.jsonl" --examples 10 --seed 42`
4) `python ssmde_verify.py`  → **ALL CHECKS PASSED**

DAY 30 — Tighten governance & scale out
----------------------------------------
**Goals:**
- **Promote** the manifest to a versioned policy repo (review, sign-offs, change log).
- **CI checks**: run `ssmde_verify.py` and `--manifest-validate` on every change.
- **Rollout playbook**: who edits manifests, who approves, how to notify operations of band window changes.
- **Observability**: dashboards show both **value** and **align**, with **band** color and SLA timers derived from the manifest’s action windows.
- **Chaining everywhere**: ensure `prev` uses the hash of the last emitted stamp to make timeline tampering visible.

----------------------------------------
Integration quick notes
----------------------------------------
**Do NOT change your classical values.** `phi((m,a)) = m` — values remain the same.
**Start with uniform weights.** Only add weighting if a domain expert asks for it.
**Keep band labels human.** “CRITICAL/AMBER/A0/A++” is fine; your org can rename.
**Use canonical JSON for hashing the content block.** Keys sorted, `,` and `:` separators tight.
